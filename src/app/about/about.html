About Dragon Ball

Dragon Ball is one of the most influential anime and manga franchises of all time. Created by Akira Toriyama in 1984, it follows the journey of Goku from his childhood to becoming one of the strongest warriors in the universe. With action, heart, humor, and powerful themes of perseverance and friendship, Dragon Ball has captured fans globally for over 40 years.
